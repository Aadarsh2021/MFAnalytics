# SEBI Official Mutual Fund Categorization (2024)

## Complete 36 Categories

### 1. EQUITY SCHEMES (11 categories)

1. Large Cap Fund
2. Mid Cap Fund  
3. Small Cap Fund
4. Large & Mid Cap Fund
5. Multi Cap Fund
6. Flexi Cap Fund
7. Value Fund / Contra Fund (either/or)
8. Focused Fund
9. Dividend Yield Fund
10. Sectoral/Thematic Fund
11. ELSS (Tax Saver)

### 2. DEBT SCHEMES (16 categories)

**Ultra Short Duration:**

1. Overnight Fund
2. Liquid Fund
3. Ultra Short Duration Fund (Macaulay duration: 3-6 months)
4. Low Duration Fund (Macaulay duration: 6-12 months)
5. Money Market Fund

**Short to Medium:**
6. **Short Duration Fund** (Macaulay duration: 1-3 years)
7. Medium Duration Fund (Macaulay duration: 3-4 years)
8. Medium to Long Duration Fund (Macaulay duration: 4-7 years)
9. Long Duration Fund (Macaulay duration: >7 years)

**Other Debt:**
10. Dynamic Bond Fund
11. Corporate Bond Fund
12. Credit Risk Fund
13. Banking & PSU Fund
14. Gilt Fund
15. Gilt Fund with 10-year constant duration
16. **Floater Fund** (65%+ in floating rate instruments)

### 3. HYBRID SCHEMES (7 categories)

1. Conservative Hybrid Fund
2. Balanced Hybrid Fund / Aggressive Hybrid Fund
3. Balanced Advantage Fund / Dynamic Asset Allocation
4. Multi Asset Allocation Fund
5. Arbitrage Fund
6. Equity Savings Fund

### 4. SOLUTION ORIENTED (2 categories)

1. Retirement Fund
2. Children's Fund

### 5. OTHER SCHEMES

1. Index Funds / ETFs
2. FoF (Fund of Funds) - Domestic
3. FoF - Overseas
